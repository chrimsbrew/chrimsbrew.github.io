Playcoin Buddy

A simple 3DS Play Coin editor.

Set your Nintendo 3DS play coins to any value from 0-300. Should work on all models in the 3DS family of systems.
I threw it together and polished it over a couple days with LovePotion, a version of the game framework Love2D.
Look for the latest release here.
